ReelPin Account and Data Deletion

Effective date: April 15, 2026

ReelPin users can request deletion of their account and associated ReelPin data by contacting reelpin.support@gmail.com.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to reelpin.support@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete my ReelPin account.
  3. Send the request from the same email address that is connected to your ReelPin account, or include that account email address in the message body.

What will be deleted

  • Your ReelPin account profile.
  • Your saved reel records and related app data associated with your account.
  • Associated push notification registration data for your account when applicable.

What may be retained

  • Limited records may be retained for legal, fraud-prevention, security, backup, or compliance reasons.
  • Operational logs may be retained for a limited period where reasonably necessary for security and service reliability.

Deletion timeline

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days. In some cases, limited backup or log data may persist for a short additional retention period before removal.

More information

For more information about how ReelPin handles data, see the ReelPin Privacy Policy.
